I created this website as a personal project for my web-design portfolio. In doing so, I am able to mix my love for foreign languages and my aspiration of being a web developer to create a basic quiz to test the progress of new learner of Japanese.

Please note that a basic understanding of hiragana and katakana are needed to complete the quiz.

This is mostly for fun, so the quiz is not too extensive, but I do hope that whoever uses this for practice is satisfied with their results:)

If you have any questions or suggestions, please contact me.



Instructions:

  1. Answer the following questions
  2. View your results and take a look at a few resources for additional help
  3. If you are not satisfied with your results, you may retake the quiz
